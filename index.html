Work Experience 2020— mtü Kolkaplika is a non-profit organization that promotes everyday creativity, work in community; supports artistic endeavours, the free flow of knowledge. Co-founded by two sisters in a garage for brooms and tractors on the day of wisdom and now consists of: (among others) Mari Kuuse, Helena Keskküla, Diana Lõhmus, Kadi Estland, Ookean and others. liina pääsuke is the co-founder and Artistic Director. 2024 Rüki Residency, residency coordinator 2019—2022 MEYER*KAINER, assisting personnel 2020—2022 City Games Vienna, office management 2013—2014 Nancy Nakamura Ideederiiul was an artist-run-space, led by poet and artist Kadi Estland, that operated from 2013 until 2015 in the (then “ghetto”) district of Kopli, Tallinn. It engaged itself with equality politics, more specifically with women’s and minorities rights, and questions of urban peripheries in its immediate locality. liina pääsuke was the 1st Assistant. 2012—2013 Estonian Academy of Arts, students’ representative in the Council of EAA 2012—2013 Estonian Academy of Arts, students’ representative 2010—2011 Navitrolla Galerii, gallery assistant 2012— Contemporary Artist, self-employed
